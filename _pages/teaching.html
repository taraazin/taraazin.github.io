I have enjoyed the opportunity to engage with different course topics and contribute to students' learning journeys. 1- Introduction to Linguistics (Undergraduate Level) - Fall 2021: - Conducted weekly tutorial sessions to review course materials and assist students with assignments. - Graded quizzes and the final exam for the course. 2- Forensic Linguistics (Undergraduate Level) - Winter 2022: - Assisted students with course materials and projects in Forensic Linguistics. - Monitored class discussions on the presented topics and directed student discussions. - Graded projects and provided feedback to students. 3- Phonetics (Undergraduate Level) - Fall 2022: - Conducted two weekly tutorials. - Taught students how to analyze sound using applications such as PRAAT. - Assisted students with assignments and provided guidance. 4- Semantics (Undergraduate Level) - Fall 2023: - I was nominated for the Outstanding TA Award at Carleton University for this course in Fall 2023.
